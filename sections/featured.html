DFCA-Research is presently administering a multi-year panel enumeration across the formerly tribal agencies now integrated into mainstream Khyber Pakhtunkhwa administration. Operations consist of scheduled household re-contact, facility-level audits, and the compilation of geo-tagged administrative records on behalf of the commissioning principal investigators.

The establishment is responsible for enumerator deployment, instrument delivery, protocol compliance, and the operational record. Substantive analysis, authorship, and external publication remain with the commissioning researchers.
