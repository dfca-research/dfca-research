Documented Engagement

Door-to-Door Census Deployment · Rural Peshawar

Between 2019 and 2020, DFCA-Research administered a full door-to-door census enumeration across 37 rural settlements in District Peshawar on behalf of a commissioning principal investigator. Operations covered 3,738 households and were conducted under a structured survey protocol with supervisor verification and geo-tagged completion records.

The establishment was responsible for enumerator training, instrument administration, routing, data upload integrity, and the operational record. Substantive interpretation of the data and any subsequent publication remain the property of the commissioning investigator.
