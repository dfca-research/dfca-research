DFCA-Research
Field Operations Support Establishment

A subsidiary research support unit of DFCA, operational since 2018. Administers Randomized Controlled Trial deployment, treatment delivery, field monitoring, and geo-tagged verifiable reporting for external principal investigators across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, the Merged Districts, and Punjab.
