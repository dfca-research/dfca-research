Establishment

A support establishment
for field-based empirical work.

DFCA-Research is a professional research support establishment operating under the DFCA umbrella. The establishment was constituted in 2018 to provide a dedicated operational unit that manages and executes field activities on behalf of principal investigators working in academic departments, policy institutes, and think tanks. We are not authors of the research we support; we are the administrative and logistical apparatus that carries it out in the field with structural discipline.

Four operational functions define our scope of service. Randomized Controlled Trial administration — the deployment of personnel specifically trained to conduct rigorous RCT surveys. Treatment administration — the delivery of field treatments according to protocol, with exact methodologies documented and reported. Field monitoring and evaluation — the construction of systems that allow researchers to actively monitor their operations as they unfold. Verifiable reporting — documentation of fieldwork using geo-tagged data to preserve complete operational transparency.

Engagements are framed as a cost-effective and accountable operational framework for gathering the empirical evidence required for academic papers, monographs, and policy development work. All field activity is subject to fixed internal protocols and documented oversight.

The establishment exists to provide researchers with reliability, structural discipline, and strict administrative honesty in the execution of field operations.

Organisational Position

Subsidiary Research Unit of DFCA

Constituted in 2018 as a designated field operations unit. Administrative and reporting functions are carried out independently of programme activity, so that operational records furnished to researchers are not influenced by the implementing entity.

Administered Geographic Scope

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Merged Districts & Punjab

Field deployment across the settled and merged districts of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and across agricultural and peri-urban districts of Punjab. Coverage is documented by district on the operational record.
