About

Rooted in context.
Held to global standards.

DFCA Research is the independent evidence and evaluation arm of Direct Focus Community Aid (DFCA), one of Pakistan's largest community-based development organisations. Formally established in 2018 as a dedicated research wing, it was built to translate DFCA's deep field infrastructure — developed over more than a decade serving communities across KPK and beyond — into rigorous, internationally credible research. Our team of globally trained consultants brings expertise across development economics, public health, governance, and social policy, applying internationally recognised methodological standards to some of Pakistan's most under-studied and underserved contexts.

Since 2018, we have delivered research and evaluation projects in partnership with Stanford University, UCLA, the European University Institute, Boston University, the University of Pittsburgh, the Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), the Centre for Development and Policy Research (CDPR), and the Mahbub Ul Haq Research Centre at LUMS. These collaborations produce policy-relevant findings that reach government departments, international donors, and programme managers across Pakistan and beyond.

We work where the evidence is thinnest — and the need is greatest.

Research that doesn't reach a policymaker's desk, a donor's strategy, or a programme manager's design is research that hasn't finished its job.

Organisational Structure
