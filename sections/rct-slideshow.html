Health Systems KPK · 2021–2023

Community Health Worker Performance RCT

A treatment arm delivering structured supervision and incentive redesign produced a 34% improvement in maternal care visit rates across 12 intervention districts — compared to no change in the control group.

2.1× Effect Size
Education Punjab · 2020–2022

Girls' Enrolment Incentive Trial

Conditional cash transfers paired with community mobilisation doubled girls' school enrolment in target villages compared to information-only control groups across 8 districts.

−18pp Reduction
WASH KPK · 2022–2024

Behaviour-Change WASH Intervention

An 18 percentage-point reduction in open defecation achieved through a structured social norms campaign delivered via community leaders — verified through unannounced spot-checks at endline.

3,200 Households
Food Security Punjab · 2021–2023

Agricultural Extension Services Trial

A three-arm RCT across 3,200 smallholder households tested the marginal returns to agronomic training, input subsidies, and market linkages on seasonal income and food security outcomes.
