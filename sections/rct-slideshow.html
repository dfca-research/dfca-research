RCT Deployments

Randomized Controlled Trials.
Administered under fixed protocol.

Selected operational records of RCT deployments administered by DFCA-Research on behalf of external principal investigators. Figures below describe the operational scale of each deployment; substantive findings remain attributable to the commissioning researcher.

12 Districts Administered
Health Systems Khyber Pakhtunkhwa · 2021–2023

Community Health Worker Performance Trial

Two-arm RCT administered across 12 intervention districts. DFCA-Research delivered the supervision and incentive treatment packages according to investigator-specified protocol, conducted baseline, midline and endline rounds, and furnished a geo-tagged completion record for every maternal-care visit captured.

8 Districts Administered
Education Punjab · 2020–2022

Girls' Enrolment Treatment Trial

Two-arm RCT covering conditional cash disbursement and community mobilisation treatments across 8 districts. Disbursements were administered under payment-log verification; enrolment outcomes were documented through registry-level enumeration and unannounced classroom verification.

1,800 Spot-Checks Logged
WASH Khyber Pakhtunkhwa · 2022–2024

Behaviour-Change Sanitation Deployment

A structured social-norms treatment was delivered through community leaders under script verification. DFCA-Research administered 1,800 unannounced endline spot-checks with geo-tagged photographic documentation to furnish an operationally verifiable endline record.

3,200 Households Administered
Food Security Punjab · 2021–2023

Agricultural Extension Treatment Trial

Three-arm RCT across 3,200 smallholder households. DFCA-Research administered the agronomic training, input-subsidy and market-linkage treatment packages on schedule and captured seasonal enumeration rounds with supervisor-audited consistency checks.

1,698 Households Enrolled
Governance & Enforcement Charsadda · 2022–2024

Electricity Subsidy & Enforcement Trial

A five-arm RCT addressing electricity theft and payment norms. DFCA-Research administered a three-wave panel survey, managed community financial resources for subsidy disbursement, and documented consumption records for 1,698 households in PESCO grid areas.
