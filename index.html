About

Rooted in context.
Held to global standards.

DFCA Research is the evidence and evaluation arm of Direct Focus Community Aid (DFCA), one of Pakistan's largest community-based development organisations, operating across 23 districts in KPK and FATA since 2007.

Our work draws on DFCA's unmatched field presence and the trust it has built with communities over two decades — translating that access into independent, scientifically credible research that informs programmes and policies at the provincial, national, and international level.

We collaborate closely with Pakistan's leading research institutions — including CERP and CDPR — and align our work with international evaluation standards to ensure our findings are both rigorously produced and practically actionable.

"Research that doesn't reach a policymaker's desk, a donor's strategy, or a programme manager's design is research that hasn't finished its job."

Organisational Structure

A subsidiary of DFCA Aid

DFCA Research operates with editorial independence under the DFCA Aid umbrella, ensuring scientific integrity while maintaining proximity to programme realities.

Methodology

How we work

Our methodology combines international standards of scientific rigour with a deep, contextually informed understanding of the communities we study. Every project is designed to produce both credible evidence and actionable recommendations.

We work in partnership with CERP and CDPR — Pakistan's foremost research organisations — and draw on DFCA's network of over 2,000 field practitioners to enable research that is operationally grounded.

Contributing to
SDG 1 — No Poverty SDG 2 — Zero Hunger SDG 3 — Good Health SDG 4 — Education SDG 5 — Gender Equality SDG 6 — Clean Water SDG 10 — Reduced Inequalities SDG 16 — Peace & Justice
01

Co-design with Communities

Research questions are shaped alongside the communities and practitioners we serve, ensuring relevance and ownership from the outset.

02

Rigorous Mixed Methods

We combine randomised controlled trials, quasi-experimental designs, and in-depth qualitative work to build multi-dimensional evidence.

03

Independent Analysis

Findings are analysed and reported independently of DFCA's programme operations, safeguarding scientific integrity and credibility.

04

Policy Translation

Every study produces a policy brief and stakeholder briefing designed for government counterparts, international donors, and practitioner audiences.

05

Open Knowledge

We publish datasets, methodological tools, and findings openly so the broader research and practitioner community can build on our work.
