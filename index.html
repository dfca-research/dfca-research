Who We Are

Research grounded in two decades of field presence

DFCA Research is the evidence and evaluation arm of Direct Focus Community Aid (DFCA), one of Pakistan's leading development NGOs. We translate deep community knowledge into actionable insights that inform policy and programme design across conflict-affected and disaster-prone regions.

Drawing on DFCA's network of over 2,000 field practitioners, we conduct rigorous mixed-methods studies, randomised evaluations, and longitudinal assessments — ensuring findings are both scientifically credible and contextually grounded.

Our Methodology

How We Work

01

Co-design with Communities

Research questions are shaped alongside the communities and practitioners we serve, ensuring relevance and ownership from day one.

02

Rigorous Mixed Methods

We combine randomised controlled trials, quasi-experimental designs, and deep ethnographic work to build robust, multi-dimensional evidence.

03

Independent Analysis

Findings are analysed and reported independently of programme operations, safeguarding scientific integrity and credibility.

04

Policy Translation

Every study produces a policy brief and stakeholder briefing designed for government partners, donors, and practitioner audiences.

05

Open Knowledge

We publish datasets, tools, and findings openly so the broader research community can build on our work.

Our goal is not just to produce research — it is to produce research that changes what happens on the ground.

DFCA Research collaborates with Pakistan's leading research institutions including CERP and CDPR to ensure our work meets the highest international standards of evidence.
